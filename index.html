Ordusion!!!

The Computer Science Department of Sri Ramakrishna College of Arts and Science is all about stirring up excitement with event "Ordusion." Every time the response from participants is off the charts! It's like the whole campus comes alive with the buzz of coding, creativity, and camaraderie. With the immense support they receive, "Ordusion" has become a must-attend affair for anyone passionate about computer science.

Paper Presentation

Rules : Each participant must submit their paper on the designated topic within the specified deadline. Late submissions will not be considered. Presentations must adhere to the allocated time limit.

Debugging

Rules : Participants must solve the given programming problems individually without any external assistance or resources. Any unauthorized tools or software will result in disqualification.

Web Designing

Rules : Designs must be original and not infringe upon any existing copyrights or trademarks.

Connections

Rules : 2 persons can be a team, 3 rounds will be conducted.

Facial Painting

Rules : Designs should be appropriate for all audiences and must not include any offensive or controversial imagery.

Fireless Cooking

Rules : Cooking ingredients must be brought by the participants. All cooking processes must be conducted without the use of fire or any heating elements.
